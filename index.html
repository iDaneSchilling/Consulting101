Types of Consulting

STRATEGY

The process of advising companies on important business decisions by providing strategic advice.

SOFTWARE

The practice of analyzing a company's software needs and creating technology to help a company perform its operations successfully.

DESIGN

Integrates design thinking and a human-centered design process into how they solve client problems.

Dane

My name is Dane and I have been a consultant for 8-years. I taught high school math after graduating from college with a degree in Computer Science. It was hard and did not pay well; so, I found a job as a developer. I found myself job hopping when the amount of responsibility and stress outpaced my paycheck and growth. Then I found consulting, it was a great fit for someone who loves to solve problems, but also feels responsible for fixing all the problems. As a consultant, I get to solve a problem and wave goodbye. Closure is a great feature.

Jason

My name is Jason Bogdanski. I first got my start in programming when I was in 7th grade making video game websites and hosting them on AOL. I enjoyed the challenge of figuring out HTML markup and how to host the website on AOL. Fast forward to my college days, I was majoring in computer science and was solving problems and building programs. When I graduated, I didn't land a job in computer programming, but as a second-tier support specialist. That job still got me used to solving complex problems and learning SQL to troubleshoot them. I eventually landed a position in computer programming and honed my skills from there. It was not until that I got a job in consulting that I saw my growth accelerate. There were several different types of projects each with their own unique set of solutions. I grew not only in computer programming, but leadership and mentoring skills. It's never a dull moment in consulting!
